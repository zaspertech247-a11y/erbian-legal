关于耳边

耳边是一款声音陪伴应用，为用户提供睡前、通勤与情绪调节等场景下的陪伴内容与 AI 实时对话。

产品特色

多元声线内容

精选温柔女声、治愈男声、元气女声等多种声线，覆盖睡前陪伴、早安叫醒、角色扮演、情景剧等场景。

AI 实时陪伴

支持与不同角色进行实时语音陪伴对话，角色会记住偏好与会话内容，带来连续的陪伴体验。

真人级朗读

基于 ElevenLabs 真人级 AI 语音合成，5 个 AI 角色与 5 种场景调性各有独立声线。

隐私优先

不收集位置、通讯录、相册；对话数据本地存储为主；上架前完整公开隐私政策。

About Earbian

Earbian is an audio companionship app providing bedtime, commute, and emotional regulation content along with real-time AI conversations.

Features

Diverse Voice Library

Carefully curated gentle female, healing male, energetic female and more voice styles spanning bedtime companionship, morning wake-up, role-play, and scenario drama.

Real-Time AI Companion

Engage in real-time voice conversations with multiple characters. They remember your preferences and conversation context for continuous companionship.

Human-Quality TTS

Powered by ElevenLabs human-quality AI voice synthesis, with distinct voice profiles for 5 AI characters and 5 scene tones.

Privacy First

We don't collect location, contacts, or photos. Conversations are stored locally. Full privacy policy disclosure before submission.
