社区准则

完整版本见 用户协议

为让所有用户在耳边获得安全、健康的陪伴体验，我们建立以下准则。一经发现违规，我们将在 24 小时内核查处置。

内容红线（一律禁止）

  • 色情、性暗示、性诱导内容
  • 暴力、血腥、自残、自杀诱导
  • 涉及未成年人的任何不适内容
  • 歧视、仇恨、辱骂言论
  • 毒品、违禁品、违法犯罪诱导

举报与处理

AI 对话气泡右下角、内容详情页右上角、主播主页右上角均有"举报"入口。提交后 24 小时内 完成人工核查：

  • 违规内容立即下架
  • 账号警告 / 临时封禁 / 永久封禁
  • 涉嫌违法犯罪的将配合执法部门

拉黑机制

在主播主页点击"屏蔽"将该创作者加入屏蔽列表，被屏蔽创作者的内容不会再出现在你的浏览动线中。可在「我的→账号与设置→已拉黑创作者」随时解除。

申诉

如对处理结果有异议，可发邮件至 zaspertech247@gmail.com，我们承诺 7 个工作日内回复。

未成年人

本服务面向 17 岁及以上用户。App 启动时强制 17+ 年龄确认。严禁伪造年龄信息。一经发现未成年用户使用，将立即注销账号并删除关联数据。

A SAFE COMPANIONSHIP ENVIRONMENT

Community Guidelines

Full version: Terms of Service

To ensure a safe and healthy companionship experience for all users, we establish these guidelines. Violations are reviewed and addressed within 24 hours.

Content Red Lines (Strictly Prohibited)

  • Pornography, sexual innuendo, or sexually suggestive content
  • Violence, gore, self-harm, or suicide encouragement
  • Any inappropriate content involving minors
  • Discriminatory, hateful, or abusive speech
  • Drugs, contraband, or incitement to crime

Reporting and Action

The "Report" entry is available in the bottom-right of AI conversation bubbles, the top-right of content detail pages, and the top-right of creator profiles. We commit to human review within 24 hours:

  • Violating content is immediately removed
  • Account warnings / temporary bans / permanent bans
  • Criminal violations are reported to law enforcement

Blocking Mechanism

Tap "Block" on a creator profile to add them to your block list. Blocked creators' content will no longer appear in your browsing experience. You can unblock anytime under "Me → Account & Settings → Blocked Creators".

Appeals

If you disagree with our moderation decision, email zaspertech247@gmail.com. We respond within 7 business days.

Minors

This service is intended for users aged 17 and above. An age verification overlay is enforced on App launch. Falsifying age information is prohibited. Discovered minor accounts will be immediately deleted along with associated data.
