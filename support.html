常见问题

1. 如何注销账号？

App 内："我的 → 账号与设置 → 滑到底部 → 点'注销账号' → 确认"。注销后所有数据（昵称、片单、订单、AI 对话记录、举报记录）将被永久删除，无法恢复。

2. 如何举报不当内容？

每条内容、每个 AI 对话回复、每个主播主页右上角都有"举报"入口。提交后我们承诺 24 小时内 完成人工核查与处置。

3. 如何屏蔽不喜欢的创作者？

进入该创作者主页，点击右上角"屏蔽"。被屏蔽的创作者内容将不再出现在你的浏览动线中。可在"账号与设置 → 已拉黑创作者"随时解除。

4. 忘记密码怎么办？

V1 测试版尚未上线找回密码功能。如忘记密码，请通过原登录方式重新创建账号，或发送邮件至 zaspertech247@gmail.com 协助处理。

5. 应用内是否有付费内容？

当前版本所有公开内容均免费收听，无任何会员订阅、单集解锁或付费时长包。未来若推出付费功能，将通过 Apple 内购统一处理。

6. AI 对话内容会被保存吗？

对话内容存储在你设备本地，不会上传我方服务器。请求转发给 Groq 与 ElevenLabs（实时生成回复与朗读音频），这些第三方承诺不留存对话超过 30 天。详见 隐私政策。

7. 试听只有 30 秒？

未解锁的内容试听上限为 30 秒。当前 V1 版本所有内容默认免费完整播放（不限 30 秒）；如未来推出付费解锁机制，未解锁状态将受 30 秒试听限制。

8. 换设备数据能同步吗？

V1 测试版数据存储于设备本地，暂不支持跨设备同步。后端服务上线后我们将提供云端同步。

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I delete my account?

In-App: "Me → Account & Settings → scroll to bottom → tap 'Delete Account' → confirm". All data (nickname, playlists, orders, AI conversation history, reports) will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

2. How do I report inappropriate content?

Every piece of content, AI response, and creator profile page has a "Report" entry in the top-right corner. We commit to human review within 24 hours of submission.

3. How do I block a creator?

Open the creator's profile page and tap "Block" in the top-right. Blocked creators' content will no longer appear in your browsing experience. You can unblock anytime under "Account & Settings → Blocked Creators".

4. I forgot my password.

Password recovery is not yet available in V1 Beta. Please re-register using your original login method, or email zaspertech247@gmail.com for assistance.

5. Are there in-app purchases?

The current version contains no in-app purchases and no subscriptions. All public content is free. Any future paid features will be processed exclusively through Apple In-App Purchase.

6. Are my AI conversations stored?

Conversation content is stored locally on your device and not uploaded to our servers. Requests are forwarded to Groq and ElevenLabs (for real-time response generation and audio synthesis). These third parties commit to retaining conversations no longer than 30 days. See Privacy Policy.

7. Why is the preview only 30 seconds?

The preview limit is 30 seconds for unlocked content. In the current V1 version, all content defaults to free full playback (no 30-second limit). If a paid unlock mechanism is introduced in the future, unlocked content will revert to a 30-second preview.

8. Does my data sync across devices?

V1 Beta stores data locally on your device and does not support cross-device sync. Cloud sync will be provided once backend services launch.
